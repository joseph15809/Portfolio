Overwatch Pinball Machine

A custom pinball game that combines electromechanical actuators and sensor-based scoring, all coordinated by an Arduino-driven control system. The machine tracks points and lives in real time, triggers sound feedback on events, and uses moving obstacles to keep gameplay engaging, with character selection and unique voice lines for each character.

How to Play

  1. Start / On-Off Power on → game enters character selection.
  2. Character Selection Choose a character using the buttons on the left side.
  3. Launch Pull/use the plunger to shoot the ball into the playfield.
  4. Flippers Use the side buttons to keep the ball on the field.
  5. Scoring Hit robots or pass through obstacles to earn points.
  6. Lose Condition / Return If the ball passes the flippers, an IR gate detects it and a solenoid returns it for the next life/round.

Flipper Actuation Circuit

A solenoid-based flipper mechanism was chosen to provide an immediate and tactile mechanical response. The driver circuit allows the microcontroller to safely control high-current solenoid actuation while maintaining fast response times.

IR Scoring & Life Detection

Infrared emitter-receiver pairs combined with comparator circuitry are used to detect scoring events and determine when the ball exits the playfield, triggering a life loss condition.

Score Display Circuit

A dedicated display driver circuit is used to show real-time score and game state information, updated continuously by the controller during gameplay.

H-Bridge Motor Control

An H-bridge circuit enables bidirectional control of the DC motor used for moving robot obstacles, allowing dynamic gameplay elements controlled by the game logic.
